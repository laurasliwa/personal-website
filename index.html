About ASTRO

ASTRO is a 4 member k-pop boy group from South Korea under the entertainment agency fantagio. Currently consisting of the members Jinjin, MJ, Eunwoo and Sanha. Their debut with 6 members was on February 23, 2016 with the extended play "Spring Up". Rocky leaving the group was announced on February 28, 2023 as a result of the expiration of his contract with the agency. On April 19, 2023 it was reported by the Seoul Gangnam Police Station that Moonbin has passed away in his home.
